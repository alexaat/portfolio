ASCII-WEB
Transform your plain text into eye-catching ASCII art with ASCII Text Converter, an efficient app built with Go (Golang). Convert text effortlessly, choose from a variety of styles, and download your creations with ease.

Features:
  • Text to ASCII Conversion: Instantly convert regular text into ASCII art.
  • Style Selection: Choose from multiple text styles to fit your needs.
  • Download Results: Save your ASCII art as a text file or zip. Experience the speed and reliability of Go while creating unique ASCII representations of your text. Perfect for designers, developers, and anyone looking to add a creative touch to their words.

GROUPIE-TRACKERS
Discover and track your favorite music artists with Groupie Trackers, an intuitive app developed in Go (Golang). Utilizing information from third-party APIs, this app presents artist data in a user-friendly interface. Easily search for artists, view their profiles, and stay updated with their latest activities.

Features:
  • Comprehensive Artist Info: Access detailed profiles and updates from third-party APIs.
  • User-Friendly Display: Clean, organized presentation of artist data.
  • Search Functionality: Quickly find and explore your favorite artist or band.
